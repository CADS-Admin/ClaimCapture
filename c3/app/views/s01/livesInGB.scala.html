Q3Do you normally live in Great Britain?

By Great Britain we mean England, Scotland and Wales. You should also answer 'Yes' if you are part of a family living overseas with HM forces.

If you live outside Great Britain, but in the European Economic Area (EEA) or Switzerland, and you have answered 'Yes' to the first two questions above, please go to www.gov.uk/carers-allowance for the latest information on whether you can get Carer’s Allowance.
